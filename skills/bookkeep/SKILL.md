## Active Bookkeeping

Organization and bookkeeping is important. `yy-mm-dd` prefixes, Markdown YAML frontmatter with `description` and `last_updated`. Metadata for text blocks, documents and context are key to infer decisions made over time and rationale at the time of writing. Raw text covers the “what”; Metadata covers the “why”. The goal is to make it possible to build a coherent and internally consistent mental story of the project’s evolution over time.
